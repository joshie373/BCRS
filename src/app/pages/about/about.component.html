About Us

Welcome to Bob's Computer Repair Shop! Bob’s Computer Repair Shop has been serving the Omaha community since 2008. It began as in home business for Bob Schneider, but quickly grew into a retail location in downtown Omaha. Our team consists of Bob Schneider (Owner), Seth Davis (Tech), Matthew Grant (Tech), & Katie Thomas (Administrative Assistant).



What We Do

At Bob's Computer Repair Shop we offer several services to help you out with your computer needs. We offer services such as password resets, spyware removal, RAM upgrades, software installations, PC Tune-up, keyboard cleaning, and disk clean-up. The team at Bob’s Computer Repair Shop is highly skilled and handles each task with professionalism and quality work. What makes Bob's Computer Repair Shop different from other computer shops is the fact that we offer lower prices compared to our competitors and we also offer exceptional customer service. We pride ourselves in putting the customer first and making sure each customer is highly satisfied with the work we completed for them.
